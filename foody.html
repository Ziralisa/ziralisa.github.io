KOREAN FOOD

Here I show you the top korean food that I really like and love the most. Because of my love towards korean food, some of the food I know how to make and till now, I always make it whenever I crave. All the food here I already tried to make myself, some is successful but some of it needs more improvement AIGUUU but the most food that I always make is tteokbokki.

Tteok-bokki : Spicy Stir-fried Rice Cake

Tteokbokki is ont of the most popular Korean street foods in korea. Also, it comes in almost endless variations. As an example, there is non-spicy tteokbokki or we called it Ganjang tteokbokki. It is suitable for someonw who can't handle well of spicy food. Next is rabokki. Rabokki is a combined words of ramen or ramyun + tteokbokki. The instant noodles are an additional bonus for volume, flavor and texture.

Hotteok

Hotteok is a flour dough pancake filled with sugar syrup inside. It’s one of the most popular street snacks in Korea. It would be more nice if a hot hot hotteok with sizzling golden syrup enjoy with a glass of milk.

Sundubu Jiggae

Sundubu jiggae is a korean stew made with soft tofu as a highlight ingredient. There are so many different ways on how to make it, one of it is the seafood version known as Haemul Sundubu Jjigae. Nonetheless, it's a refreshing, delicious and very comforting stew for everyone to try and enjoy.

Bungeo-Ppang : Korean Fish Shaped Pastry

Bungoeppang is a korean name for Japanese taiyaki, a fish shaped pastry that is particularly popular in winter streets of korea. This fish shaped pastry are often filled with something inside. During the old days, red bean paste is the only available fillings, but nowdays it have more choices to choose.
